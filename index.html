About

Data Scientist with experience using Python, SQL, machine learning, data analysis, research, and experimental design. I have always loved to learn new things and share what I have learned. When I was a kid I would read the encyclopedia instead of playing video games. I college I studied Family Life, with an emphasis on Human Development (think Developmental Psychology). What I appreciate most about that time is that I learned to value the scientific method and have robust skepticism in the acquisition of knowledge. After graduation, when my career plans had to change, I started studying Software Engineering, I kept on hearing about Data Science as I studied, and it really piqued my interest. I love Data Science as I love learning. Data Science is rigorous, yet nuanced. Demanding, yet also creative. It is a gateway to hidden knowledge and the communication of it. Connect with me you are looking for someone who will never stop learning and improving until the desired results are achieved. I am an excellent team player. Specialties: Data Cleaning, Data Analysis, Research, Modeling, Machine Learning.
